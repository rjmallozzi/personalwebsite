About

Rico Mallozzi is a Partner at 359 Capital, an early-stage venture capital firm with approximately $300M in assets under management, investing across consumer and consumer-adjacent technology companies. He focuses on companies building at the intersection of AI, fintech, digital health, and consumer social.

Before 359 Capital, Rico was at Sapphire Ventures, one of the largest venture firms globally. He began his career starting a renewable energy integration firm called GreenLight Energy, before spending time working across Strategy, M&A, and Business Development at enterprise technology companies such as SAP and Cisco.

Rico holds an MBA from the University of Notre Dame and a BS from Bryant University where he played football. He is passionate about technology and the positive impacts it will have on society.
